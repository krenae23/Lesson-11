KaelasGraphics

Kaela is a senior in college for Graphic Design. She decided to become a graphic design major in her second semester of college. She was originally a digital art major. Kaela learned about Graphic Design and fell in love with the field.

Scroll down to learn more about KaelasGraphics. If you want to see some of her work, check out her social media accounts.

Finding out about Graphic Design

In her first semester of college, Kaela was a major in Digital Art. For one of her classes, she had to design packaging for a chocolate bar. While creating the design, she fell in love with the process. After that, she did a lot of research on Graphic Design. She eventually ended up changing her major.

Journey through College

During her two years of college, she has created many designs that she is proud of. There is one in particular that she is most proud of. In April 2022, Kaela designed a logo for the NC3ADL Conference. Her logo will be presented in the conference in 2023.

Future Plans

Kaela plans to graduate with an Associates degree for Graphic Design in May 2023. She hopes to find a job for a design company. Her end goal is to start designing merchandise for big brands/celebrities and/or create album art.
